Description

In Scrabble, you can have 35,000 letter combinations (words) with just seven letters. To limit this range, use the power of the crossword. From the provided file, read a random line that consists of seven letters, followed by words to form and their positions, and ACR or DWN representing the direction.

For a random line, fill the cell in the crossword with letters as described in the line. The file name for the crossword scrabble file should be passed as a command-line argument â€” for example, crosswordPuzzle.txt

Objectives
  • Read one random line from the file and display the content of the line on the crossword grid every time users select the Shuffle command. Don't display the same line twice.
  • If Shuffle is executed, and there are no lines to choose from, print No more puzzles. Bye. and terminate the application.

The file format is as follows - note that in the file, unlike the Place command, the coordinates of words are given in decimal format, not hexadecimal:

IDOETIN edition 5 7 ACR iodine 9 7 ACR noted 10 3 ACR ident 10 12 ACR end 7 10 ACR Tenioid 4 7 DWN

nefisdr fined 4 9 ACR fern 8 6 ACR serif 10 9 ACR Friends 4 9 DWN
